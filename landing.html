The process

HOW
IT
WORKS.

01
Grant camera & location
One tap. Your camera becomes a window into the map layer. No app store, no install — runs in your browser.
02
We fetch your surroundings
Live data pulled from OpenStreetMap within a 1km radius — up to 100 places across 10 categories in under 2 seconds.
03
Point. See. Explore.
Your compass heading determines what's on screen. Turn left or right and pins shift with you in real time. Far places float high, near ones sit low.
04
Filter by category
Tap a category chip to focus only on what you need — all other pins disappear. Switch instantly, no reload.

What's inside

BUILT
DIFFERENT.

No app. No subscription. No ads. Just your camera, your GPS, and OpenStreetMap — rendered as a spatial layer in real time.

📡
Live OSM data
Pulls directly from OpenStreetMap's Overpass API. Always current, community-maintained, globally available.
🧭
Real compass heading
Uses device orientation sensors — gyroscope, magnetometer — to track exactly which direction you're facing.
🔍
Overlap-aware rendering
When pins cluster, crowded ones collapse to icon-only bubbles. No label soup. Always readable.
🎨
Category colors
Each category has its own color. Scan the scene visually — no need to read every label to find food vs. banking.
📱
No install needed
Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Open in any mobile browser. Share a link. Done. No App Store friction.
🌐
Works everywhere
OSM covers the entire planet. Dubai, Tokyo, Lagos — if it's on the map, it shows up in your camera.
